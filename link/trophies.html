Milan Trophies and Cabinet :

AC Milan trophies are the club's sense of pride. Since its founding in 1899 by two English expatriates, the club has grown to become one of the dominant forces in the Italian football scene, the European scene, and the world. Winning its first title just two years after its founding, AC Milan has quite an impressive trophy count displaying some of football's illustrious moments.FACTS ABOUT AC MILAN'S PALMARÈS - How many titles do AC Milan have in their trophy cabinet? The Rossoneri have won 49 trophies: 19 Scudetti, 5 Coppe Italia, 7 Supercoppe Italiane, 7 Champions League, 5 Supercoppe Europee, 2 Coppe delle Coppe, 3 Coppe Intercontinentali and 1 FIFA Club World Cup. - When was the Club’s last trophy? The Rossoneri's last trophy came on 22 May 2022, when Stefano Pioli's men won the Scudetto. - How many Champions League trophies have AC Milan won? The Rossoneri have won seven Champions Leagues: In 1962/63, 1968/69, 1988/89, 1989/90, 1993/94, 2002/03 and 2006/07. - In what year was the Rossoneri’s first Champions League? AC Milan’s first European trophy was in 1962/63 (as it was then, the European Cup), on 22 May 1963 at Wembley Stadium, London. They beat Benfica 2-1 in the final, thanks to a José Altafini brace. - How many different opponents have AC Milan beaten in the Champions League final? The Rossoneri have beaten six opponents in Champions League finals: Benfica in 1962/63 and 1989/90, Ajax in 1968/69, Steaua Bucarest in 1988/89, Barcelona in 1993/94, Juventus in 2002/03 and Liverpool in 2006/07. - How many European Super Cups have the Club won? The Rossoneri have won five Super Cups: in 1989 and 1994 at San Siro, 1990 in Bologna, 2003 in Monaco and 2007 in Seville. - How many Scudetti have AC Milan won? The Rossoneri have won nineteen Scudetti: 1901, 1906, 1907, 1950/51, 1954/55, 1956/57, 1958/59, 1961/62, 1967/68, 1978/79, 1987/88, 1991/92, 1992/93, 1993/94, 1995/96, 1998/99, 2003/04, 2010/11 and 2021/22. - What year was the Rossoneri's first Scudetto? The Club became Champions of Italy for the first time on 5 May 1901, beating Genoa 3-0 in their hometown in the Final. The Coach was Herbert Kilpin, the founding father of the Rossoneri. - How many Supercoppe Italiane have the Club won? The Rossoneri have captured seven Supercoppe Italiane: 1988, 1992, 1994 and 2004 at San Siro, 1993 in Washington, 2011 in Pechino, 2016 in Doha. - How many times have the side been World Champions? AC Milan have held that title on four occasions: 1969 in Buenos Aires, 1989 and 1990 in Tokyo and 2007 in Yokohama. - Who is the Clubs most successful coach? Nereo Rocco was the Rossoneri’s most successful coach. He was in charge four times: from 1961 to 1963, 1967 to 1972 and as technical director from 1972 to 1974, from 1975 to 1977. In total, he won 10 trophies. - How many Ballon d’Ors have AC Milan players won? Six players have won the accolade whilst at the Club: Gianni Rivera in 1969, Ruud Gullit in 1987, Marco van Basten in 1988, 1989 and 1992, George Weah in 1995, Andriy Shevchenko in 2004 and Ricardo Kaká in 2007. - What was our longest streak of trophy-winning seasons? The longest such streak in consecutive years lasted 9 seasons: from 1987/88 to 1995/96. - What is the highest number of trophies AC Milan have won in a calendar year? The most trophies the Club has won in a year was four, in 1989 (including the 1988/89 and the 1989/90 seasons). This period saw the Rossoneri capture a Supercoppa Italiana, a Champions League, a Supercoppa Europea and a Coppa Intercontinentale.
